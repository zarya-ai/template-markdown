# Goals & Wants Templates

Templates for goal-setting, personal development planning, and tracking aspirations.

## Templates in This Category

- **goal-template.md** — Individual goal planning
- **smart-goals.md** — SMART goals framework
- **personal-development.md** — Personal growth planning
- **wish-list.md** — Wish list and aspirations

## Usage

Use these templates to clarify your goals, create actionable plans, and track progress toward your objectives.
