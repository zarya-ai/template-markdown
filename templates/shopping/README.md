# Shopping Templates

Templates for organizing shopping, tracking inventory, and managing budgets.

## Templates in This Category

- **shopping-list.md** — General purpose shopping list
- **grocery-list.md** — Grocery shopping specific template
- **inventory-tracker.md** — Inventory and stock tracking
- **budget-tracker.md** — Shopping budget and expense tracking

## Usage

Use these templates to organize your shopping, track what you have, and manage spending.
